### **Core Concept: Topic vs. Subject**

Think of **„ÅØ (wa)** as a spotlight on a stage. It shines on the main theme of your sentence and says, "Okay everyone, we're talking about THIS now." The focus is on what you say *about* the topic.

Think of **„Åå (ga)** as a pointing finger. It singles out a specific person or thing and says, "**This one!**" It answers an unasked question like "Who?" or "Which one?" The focus is on the person/thing being identified.
